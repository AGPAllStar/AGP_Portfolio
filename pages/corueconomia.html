Game Description

This game was developed in cooperation with Enxeñeria Sen Fronteiras. The purpose of the game is to teach players about the challenges of surviving working life in Galicia today.

In this game you play the part of a recent university graduate. As a ex-student, you select a work and a flat and you have to survive to modern life. You have to life in A Coruña trying not to run out of money and to keep your mental health, diet, sleep and social status stable.

As the days go by, events will occur that will bring about changes in the player’s status. Some will even force the player to make important decisions.

Tasks performed

Events queue

I developed a system that generates a event list each day of the game. The events generated depend on the weekday and a degree of randomness.

Menu flow

I defined the menu flow of the game. You have to select first your flat and work to begin play. Also when the following event is dispatched, his information is shown on screen with its effects on player.

Interface desing

I designed the main HUD of the game, adjusting its components to show de information clearly.

2D Art

I've created almost all the 2D sprites for the game. I used a simple style to make it clear what they mean.
